Sociology with Pop.Ed.

SUBJECT OVERVIEW

In this course, you will delve into the realms of esteemed sociological theories, namely functionalism, conflict theory, symbolic interactionism, and feminism. These theories serve as invaluable tools to scrutinize and comprehend intricate social phenomena. Moreover, you will extensively examine a myriad of social institutions, ranging from the familial, educational, and religious spheres to the political and economic domains. By dissecting their profound influence on both individual and collective behaviors and experiences, you will gain a profound understanding of the intricate tapestry that comprises our society.
