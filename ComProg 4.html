ComProg 4(Web Programming & Design)

SUBJECT OVERVIEW

In this course, you will acquire the necessary skills to effortlessly manipulate the arrangement, hues, typographies, and overall aesthetic of your web pages through the art of CSS. Moreover, you will gain proficiency in organizing web content using HTML, encompassing the creation of captivating headings, eloquent paragraphs, comprehensive lists, seamless links, interactive forms, and the seamless integration of visually captivating multimedia elements such as striking images and captivating videos.
